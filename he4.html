Climate And Statistics

Chapter 12 Risk Projections

Author: Author

Supervisor: Helmut Kuechenhoff

Degree: Bachelor

In many studies, the effects of climate change in the future is discussed. In a tutorial paper (Vicedo-Cabrera, Sera, and Gasparrini (2019)) , statistical methods for modelling future risks due to climate devlopement are presented. In a two other papers paper the future effects of ozone and heat related are presented (Domingo et al. (2024),Chen et al. (2019)).
